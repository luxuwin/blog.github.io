--- title: Easy Crisp Biscuit date: 2015-12-20 19:32:25.000000000 -05:00 categories: - Baking - Food --- This is a Chinese traditional cookie 桃酥. I learned this from a good friend. It is a very easy recipe, requires very basic materials, and takes less than 20 minutes to prepare. Materials: Vegetable oil 120g Sugar 100g Egg 1 Cake powder (or all purpose flour) 300g Baking Soda 3g Recipe: Mix the oil and sugar, and add beaten egg. Add sifted flour and baking soda and use hand to mix evenly. Divide and shape into small balls (about 1 inch in diameter), place on oven plate and press with palm. Top with some sesame to decorate (optional). Bake at 325F for 18 minutes - Done!
